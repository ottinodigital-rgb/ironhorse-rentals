What You'll Need

Rental Requirements

Meet these basics and we'll have you on the road fast.

Driver & ID

  • Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license)
  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • Phone number for contact during rental period
  • Signed rental agreement at time of pickup

Vehicle & Towing Setup

  • Vehicle with adequate tow rating for chosen trailer
  • Correct ball size: 2" for utility / 2-5/16" for dump and hauler
  • Working 4-pin or 7-pin trailer connector
  • Safety chains and functioning brake lights

Payment

  • Full rental fee due at time of pickup
  • Cash or electronic payment accepted
  • Damage deposit may be required
  • Cleaning or damage fees charged at return if applicable

Trailer Return

  • Return by the agreed time on your return date
  • Trailer must be returned clean and free of debris
  • No hazardous or illegal materials
  • Late returns billed at daily rate per additional day

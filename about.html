Iron Horse Rentals was built on the idea that East Texans deserve access to quality, well-maintained trailers without the big-box hassle. We're your neighbors — and we run it that way.

Who We Are

Built in Paris, TX.
Built for Hard Work.

Iron Horse Rentals is a locally owned and operated trailer rental business serving Paris, Texas and the surrounding East Texas region.

We started with a simple belief: people around here work hard, and they deserve equipment that works just as hard. Whether you're a contractor hauling debris, a rancher moving equipment, or a homeowner cleaning up after a storm — you need a trailer that's ready to go when you are.

Our fleet of Maxx-D and Lamar trailers is maintained to a high standard before every rental. No surprises, no runarounds. Just well-built equipment, fair pricing, and a straight deal from people you can actually call.

We're proud to serve our community and we take that responsibility seriously. When you rent from Iron Horse, you're not calling a 1-800 number — you're calling a real person in Paris, TX who wants to see your job get done right.
